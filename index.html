Hi Asyl! Are you ready to learn all about the verb to be? It's one of the most important verbs in English, and it's super easy to use once you know the rules. "To be" is a "state of being" verb. It tells us who someone is, what they are feeling, or where they are.

Part 1: The Forms of "To Be"

Positive Forms (+)

This is how you say something is true. You can also use short forms, called contractions (like "I'm" or "She's"), to sound more natural!

Subject Verb Example
Iam / 'mI am a volleyball player. üèê
